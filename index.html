About Me

I've been interested in computers my entire life and worked out how to make my first video game when I was 11. Since then, a large majority of my spare time has been dedicated to understanding the world with a particular focus in computers and programming. I've been programming for over 8 years now thanks to my interest in game design. As of 2019, I am a University student studying a Bachelor of IT, majoring in Digital Media. My first job involved helping make the game Experience, and I currently teach kids to code during the school holidays at CodeCamp.
