About Me

I've been interested in computers and the world in general my entire life and worked out how to make my first video game when I was 11. Since then, a large majority of my spare time has been dedicated to understanding the world with a particular focus in computers and programming. I've been programming for over 5 years now thanks to my interest in game design. As of 2017, I am a highschool student in year 11, studying at the ASMS (Australian Science and Mathematics School). I was born on the 11th of Aug. 2000. My first job involved helping make the game Experience, and I currently teach kids to code during the school holidays at CodeCamp.
