Communicating with ICT:
Whilst being actively involved with the making of 'Experience' - The App, I had to have some form of quick response communication to my team in out of school hours as there was no way that we could have finished the app within school times only. We made use of Slack team conduct all of our online communication, helping us to collaborate effectively by being able to ask questions and get help, share ideas and what we have been working on at any time in the development of the game, and to plan and organise meetings and checkpoints that needed to be reached and updating them accordingly as to be kept in a realistic timeframe. This was a major advantage because it was hard to get everyone to be together at one time due to different year levels, ie. the year 12s often had commitments or tests that they had to do inside of school hours. The only disadvantage to this is that most of the communications were restricted to text, and I found it difficult to explain certain concepts and ideas through typing over the internet. For a solution to this, we ended up setting regular meetings that we would all attend as it got even harder to communicate in person after the year 12s in our team had finished high school.

Managing and Operating with ICT:
Early in 2018, I created a Discord bot, which when added to a users server, it will respond to various commands the user types in the text channel for entertainment purposes. From the beginning, I have had to manage this discord bot so there were no bugs, but also because new features have been added over time. To operate this bot, I've been managing my own VPS (virtual private server), where I update the bot's code from my main computer to the cloud on the VPS and it then updates in the Discord app to allow for new features and big fixes to become live.

Applying social and ethical protocols and practises when using ICT:
Apart of applying social and ethical protocols and practices whilst using ICT is being able to understand, accept, and follow the terms and conditions of various websites. Recently, there was a new regulation put into affect as of May 25th, basically stating that people have a right to access all of the information gathered by any particular company, that they will be notified of any potential breach of their personal data, as well as the companies needing to provide an intelligible and easily accessed form using clear and plain language. This is so the consumer will be able to read and understand the terms and conditions that they are agreeing too. I have been able to complete this part fo the ICT capability by reading through, agreeing to, and following the terms and conditions of every website I have signed up for using ICT. For my Research Project I conducted a survey, which helped me to gain a generalised opinion on certain ideas and ways I was going about designing the game I succeeded in doing. During the process of making the survey, I thought about ways I could overcome some ethical circumstances that when not met, could make certain people not want to participate in the research. I overcame this by stating at the beginning that there would be privacy protection and anonymity if I were to speak about the results obtained. Part of this was to also make sure that no one else was involved in looking at the individual data I retrieved other than myself. For my UES topic in semester 1 of 2018, I wrote a piece about RFID (radio-frequency identification) and its impacts in various workplaces and the general society as well as how it could potentially impact our future; being a major role in ICT nowadays, I believe I chose my topic well.
